THE ARTIST

Biography

PacMan SD also known as Simphiwe Parkim is a hip-hop recording artist from Ngwenya Eswatini, recognized for his commanding delivery, lyrical precision, and authentic storytelling. Emerging from the country’s growing rap movement, he represents a new generation of artists focused on elevating Swazi hip-hop to international standards.

His music reflects ambition, perseverance, and lived experience, delivered through confident flows and modern production. With a clear artistic vision and consistent output, PacMan SD continues to build a strong presence within the Southern African hip-hop landscape.

Driven by passion and discipline, PacMan SD is steadily positioning himself as an artist to watch—both locally and beyond Eswatini’s borders.

Style & Influences

PacMan SD’s sound blends contemporary hip-hop with raw lyrical content rooted in real-life experiences. His music balances street-inspired energy with polished production, making it accessible while remaining authentic.

Often compared to Qibho Intellectual for his sharp lyricism and Siswati punchlines.

Career Highlights

  • Host and Headliner of #FillUpPrimavera (2025), gathering over 600 people at Ngwenya Oshoek.
  • Host of The Primavera Local Awards 2025.
  • Founder of Gas House Music.
  • Makasane: Song Of The Year 2025 Award.
